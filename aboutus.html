The Kitchen

About Us

"Mattam" means "a specific change" is a philosophical word in Malayalam (South Indian) language that wakes up all during an unprecedented time in the modern era. We at The Kitchen take that change that sits right at your heart is food and wellbeing. We have pushed ourselves to find a better and safe mode in all that we take pride in fulfilling everyone taste buds at your doorstep forever, making sure a fresh meal creates a unique way of eating, healthy lives to be more beneficial. During the pandemic in 2020, The Kitchen restaurant was aimed to establish an idea to serve the meal to your doorstep for needy customers. Today, our virtual platform is fully furnished all around Kent. We have an excellent professional background, Head chef, and a big team to run The Kitchen. At our food business, we wish to stick with high standards, fresh ingredients, cooked fresh, served on time, and caterer all dietary needs with unique flavors and spices to be the best on your dining table. Our menu features Asian favorites, Burgers and salads, Mediterrane meat platters, and a selection of mouth-watering desserts with an authentic touch. We are looking forward to establishing more new ventures virtually and physically in and around the UK in the coming years.
